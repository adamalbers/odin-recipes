Cheeseburger

Description

Best cheeseburger ever.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger 80/20 or 90/10
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Heat a grill or stainless steel pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Sear the hamburger until a brown crust forms, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Flip the burger and cook until center reaches 120 F.
  4. Place cheese on top of burger and continue to cook until center of burger reaches 135 F and cheese is melted.
  5. Place burger on bun and top with condiments.
