Italian Sausage and Peppers

Description

Italian sausage, peppers, and onions.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Sweet Peppers
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the sausage on all sides in a hot skillet.
  2. Transfer the sausage to a plate and slice.
  3. Cook the garlic and onions in melted butter for about three minutes, then add the peppers and dried herbs.
  4. Stir in the wine and cook until the veggies are tender.
  5. Return the sausage to the skillet and simmer, covered, until sausage is cooked through.
